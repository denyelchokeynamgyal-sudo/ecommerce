Ordering

Delivery

Within Thimphu: 1–2 days. Other dzongkhags: 3–5 days via domestic courier. We confirm the exact timeline when you order. For urgent orders, let us know and we'll do our best to prioritise.

Products

Yes. All our jerseys are premium player-grade quality with authentic club crests, official sponsor logos, and professional fabric — the same quality worn by players. We source from trusted suppliers and don't sell counterfeits.

Machine wash cold (30°C max) on a gentle cycle. Do not tumble dry — hang dry in the shade. Do not iron directly on crests or printed areas. Turn inside out before washing to preserve colours and badge quality.

Returns & Exchanges

Yes — size exchanges within 7 days of delivery, as long as the jersey is unworn and in its original condition. Message us on WhatsApp and we'll arrange the swap. We recommend using our Size Guide below before ordering to get the right fit first time.
